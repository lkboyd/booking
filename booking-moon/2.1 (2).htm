Dr.Sosen Botani, DDS

Sosen Botani, DDS
General & Cosmetic Dentist

Dr Botani qualified in Sweden in
1999 and has been practicing general
dentistry in London since 2000. She is
the practice principal and she holds a
Certificate in Advanced Aesthetic
Dentistry from UCL Eastman Dental
Institute.

With 12 years in dentistry Dr Botani has extensive experience as a general practitioner. Her special interests are preventative and minimally invasive cosmetic dentistry. She is committed to continued professional development and strives to provide her patients with the best and most advanced treatments that modern dentistry has to offer. This is in addition to the excellent backup she receives from the various highly-skilled specialists at B Dental.

Dr Botani is also a certified Invisalign and Inman Aligner practitioner.
